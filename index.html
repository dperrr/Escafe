Matcha Cake

Elevate your dessert experience with a Matcha Cake, a delectable creation that marries the elegance of cake with the bold flavors of matcha. The soft layers of cake are infused with the distinct aroma and vibrant green color of matcha, resulting in a delightful harmony of sweetness and earthy undertones.

Matcha Ice Cream

Indulge in the creamy delight of Matcha Ice Cream, a refreshing frozen treat infused with the rich essence of finely ground green tea leaves. Its vibrant color and distinct matcha flavor create a harmonious blend of sweetness and earthiness, making each spoonful a delightful experience.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 tablespoons matcha green tea powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 4 large egg yolks
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Matcha Latte

Immerse yourself in the comforting warmth of a Matcha Latte, a comforting beverage that combines the soothing qualities of milk with the invigorating taste of matcha. With its smooth texture and balanced flavor, this latte offers a serene moment of relaxation and a gentle energy boost.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon matcha green tea powder
  • 2 tablespoons hot water (not boiling)
  • 1 cup milk (dairy or non-dairy)
  • Sweetener (honey, agave, sugar) to taste (optional)
